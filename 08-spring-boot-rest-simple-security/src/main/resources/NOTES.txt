## After adding rest.base-path property and @RepositoryRestResource, Custom REST ENDPOINTS

1. GET - http://localhost:8080/our-api/our-employees
2. GET - http://localhost:8080/our-api/our-employees/{employeeId}
3. POST - http://localhost:8080/our-api/our-employees
4. PUT - http://localhost:8080/our-api/our-employees/{employeeId}
5. DELETE - http://localhost:8080/our-api/our-employees/{employeeId}
